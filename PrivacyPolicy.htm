Uses of Information

WE WILL NEVER SELL YOUR EMAIL ADDRESS, OR ANY OTHER PERSONAL INFORMATION, TO ANY THIRD PARTY.  EVER.

Credential Connection may provide you the option to join Credential Connection’s Staffing Database by authorizing the release of your contact information and evaluation results derived from Credential Connection’s online application process to potential employers and other institutions.  Except as described in this section or to the extent you have indicated opted-in to joining the Credential Connection’s Staffing Database, we will not disclose your personal information to any third party without notifying you of our intent to share the information and giving you an opportunity to prevent your information from being shared.

From time to time, we may partner with companies based on the interests of our users. These partner companies will never see your email address or any other information that could identify you or be used to contact you directly.

Any exceptions to this policy of sharing your name, address or email address with a partner company will be done only with your permission.

Credential Connection reserves the right to disclose information when required by law.

We occasionally use other companies to perform services necessary to our operations. In the course of providing these services, those companies may have access to your personal information. By contractual agreement, those companies must treat your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. However, we will not be liable for any damages that may result from the misuse of your personal information by these companies.

Credential Connection may use your IP address to help diagnose problems with Credential Connection’s server and to administer Credential Connection’s web site. Your IP address may also be used to help identify you and your online profile and to gather broad demographic information.

Credential Connection’s site's registration form requires users to give Credential Connection contact information (such as your name and email address) and demographic information (such as your zip code, age, or income level). Credential Connection uses customer contact information from the registration form to send the user information about Credential Connection and promotional material from some of Credential Connection’s partners. The customer's contact information is also used to contact the user when necessary. Credential Connection may use demographic and/or profile data to tailor the visitor's experience on Credential Connection’s site, show the visitor content that Credential Connection thinks you may be interested in, and display content according to your preferences. The demographic and profile data will be shared with advertisers ONLY on an aggregate basis.  

We may also disclose, on an anonymous basis, statements made by our customers.

Security

Credential Connection uses industry-standard technologies when transferring and receiving consumer data exchanged between Credential Connection and other companies to help ensure its security. This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under Credential Connection’s control. Credential Connection’s servers are backed up regularly and protected by security systems.

Cookies

“Cookies” are small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. Credential Connection’s site uses cookies to keep track of your session, shopping cart, and advertising delivery. Credential Connection may also use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests and to save your password. Credential Connection may use an outside ad company to display ads on Credential Connection’s site. These ads may also contain cookies. While Credential Connection uses cookies in other parts of Credential Connection’s web site, cookies received with banner ads are collected by Credential Connection’s ad company and Credential Connection does not have access to this information.

Credential Connection cannot control these links and Credential Connection is not responsible for any content appearing on these sites.
